Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a great meal for any meal. My family loves it for breakfast, lunch or dinner.

Ingrediants

  • White or Brown Rice
  • Mixed Vegtables (peas, carrots, etc.)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onions (fresh or dried)

Steps

  1. Start with heading meat.
  2. Add vegtables and cook until slightly tender.
  3. Add cooked rice from previous meal.
  4. Cook until rice is soft and breaks up evenly. It should be slightly browned but not burned.
  5. While cooking, season to taste.
