Liver and Onions

Description

Liver and onions is easy and taste delicious if it is cooked correctly.

Ingrediants

  • Beef livers
  • One medium yellow onion
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Saute onions on med-high heat. Season with salt and pepper until onions are translusant.
  2. Set cooked onions aside in a seperate dish.
  3. Cook and season liver with salt and peper. Do not over cook meat, it should be medium.
  4. Serve immediately with onions placed over the top or on the side.
