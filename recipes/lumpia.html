Lumpia

Description

Lumpia is enjoyed by many Filipinos and non-filipinos throught the world. There are several differnt styles of lumpia, and most are delicious.

Ingrediants

  • Lumpia wrapper
  • Filling consisting of:
    • Mixed vegtables
    • Meat
    • Spices: Salt, soy sauce, pepper, fish sauce, etc.

Steps

  1. Cook meat and season to taste (for less salt, do not season until combined with vegtables)
  2. Cook Mixed Vegtables
  3. Combine Vegtables and Meat and simmer on stove for 5 minutes, season to taste
  4. After seasoning, allow to sit and combine flavors for 5 - 10 minutes until cooled.
  5. Drain in colendar and place colendar back on pot to allow for full draining.
    6. >
  6. While vegtables and meat filling are cooling, seperate or cook lumpia wrappers
  7. Take a single lumpia wrapper and place 1-2 tablespoons of filling on lumpia wrapper halfway between the center and the edge towards you.
  8. Take the edge towards you and start to role it over the filling.
  9. Role about a third of the way and then fold the sides in (like an egg role or burrito)
  10. Finish roling and set aside for frying later
  11. Fry in favorite oil, best if mild flavored like canola, but EVOO works too.
